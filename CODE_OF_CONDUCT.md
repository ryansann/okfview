# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want okfview to be a focused, respectful project where people can report issues,
propose changes, and collaborate without harassment or abuse.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Keep feedback specific to the work.
- Respect differing experience levels, backgrounds, and viewpoints.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Public or private abuse, including sustained disruption of project spaces.
- Publishing someone else's private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block people
whose conduct harms the project or its contributors. Enforcement decisions are made at
the maintainers' discretion and should fit the severity and context of the behavior.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer listed in the project repository.
Please include links, screenshots, or other context when available.
